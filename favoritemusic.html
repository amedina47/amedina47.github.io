My Favorite Music

I enjoy a wide variety of different music genres. A couple of my favorite genres, however, would be: indie rock, neopsychedelic, and dream pop. For each of these genres, I'll list some of my favorite music artists.

Dream Pop

My favorite dream pop artist is Cuco. He is Mexican-American and is from Hawthorne, California. His music means a lot to me as a Mexican-American. I find his music to be very original and listening to his music always puts me in a good mood. Here is the link to my favorite song by him.
